Complaint Handling Guidelines

1. Complaint Resolution Time
- Standard complaints are typically resolved within 3-5 business days
- Urgent complaints are prioritized and resolved within 1-2 business days
- Complex issues may take up to 7 business days

2. Complaint Categories
- Product Quality Issues
- Delivery Delays
- Packaging Problems
- Customer Service
- Billing Issues

3. Required Information for Complaints
- Customer Name
- Contact Number
- Email Address
- Detailed Description of the Issue
- Order/Reference Number (if applicable)

4. Complaint Status Updates
- Customers receive email notifications for status changes
- Status can be checked using the complaint ID
- Support team contacts within 24 hours of complaint registration

5. Escalation Process
- Level 1: Customer Service Representative
- Level 2: Team Lead
- Level 3: Department Manager
- Level 4: Senior Management

6. Refund and Compensation Policy
- Full refund for undelivered items
- Partial refund for damaged items
- Compensation for delays exceeding 7 days
- Replacement for defective products

7. Contact Information
- Customer Service: 24/7 support
- Email: support@company.com
- Phone: 1-800-COMPLAINT
- Live Chat: Available on website

8. Documentation Requirements
- Order confirmation
- Photos of damaged items
- Communication records
- Delivery proof

9. Quality Assurance
- All complaints are reviewed by QA team
- Regular feedback analysis
- Continuous improvement process
- Customer satisfaction surveys

10. Privacy and Data Protection
- Customer information is kept confidential
- Data is stored securely
- Information is used only for complaint resolution
- Customers can request data deletion



Customer Service Knowledge Base

Common Issues and Solutions:

Password and Login Issues:
- If you forgot your password, click on 'Forgot Password' on the login page
- For login problems, ensure you're using the correct email address
- Clear your browser cache and cookies if you're experiencing login issues
- Make sure your caps lock is off when entering your password
- Contact support if you're still unable to access your account

Account Management:
- To update your profile information, go to Account Settings
- For account suspension or deactivation issues, contact our support team
- To change your email address, verify your identity first
- Account recovery usually takes 24-48 hours
- Keep your account information up to date for security purposes

Technical Problems:
- Clear your browser cache and cookies for most technical issues
- Try using a different browser if you're experiencing problems
- Ensure your internet connection is stable
- Disable browser extensions that might interfere with our service
- Update your browser to the latest version
- Try accessing the service from an incognito/private browsing window

Billing and Payment:
- Check your account dashboard for detailed billing information
- Payment issues are usually resolved within 2-3 business days
- We accept major credit cards and PayPal
- For refund requests, contact our billing department
- Billing cycles run monthly from the date of signup
- You can download invoices from your account dashboard

General Support:
- Our support team is available 24/7 to help you
- For urgent issues, please call our helpline: 1-800-SUPPORT
- Email support is available at support@company.com
- Live chat is available during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM EST)
- Check our FAQ section for quick answers to common questions
- Response time for email support is typically 2-4 hours

Privacy and Security:
- We take your privacy and data security seriously
- Never share your login credentials with anyone
- Report suspicious account activity immediately
- We will never ask for your password via email or phone
- Enable two-factor authentication for added security
- Review your account activity regularly

Service Features:
- Our platform offers comprehensive complaint management
- Track your complaint status in real-time
- Receive email notifications about complaint updates
- Access your complaint history anytime
- Export your complaint data when needed
- Multiple communication channels available for follow-up

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):

Q: How can I track my order?
A: You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'Orders' section. If you need further assistance, contact our support team.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Please visit our Returns page or contact support for more details.

Q: How do I update my account information?
A: Log in, go to 'Account Settings', and update your information. If you need help, our support team is here for you.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us 24/7 at support@company.com, call 1-800-SUPPORT, or use the live chat on our website.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and UPI. Check your account dashboard for more details.

Q: How do I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement from your account dashboard. For urgent cancellations, contact support immediately.

Q: Where can I find more help?
A: Check our FAQ section or contact our support team for quick answers and assistance.
